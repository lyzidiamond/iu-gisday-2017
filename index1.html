My theory: it is helpful to know a. vocabulary and b. the problem space. Knowing the vocabulary will help you search for things and recognize them in the future. Knowing the problem space will help you connect the dots quicker and problem solve for yourself.
Therefore, this talk is: a. the challenges of making maps for web and mobile, and b. how we solve those challenges. The plan is to introduce the problem space, then talk about the tools we're building. Problem space and vocabulary.
